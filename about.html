About Homewood Snowboards :

Company Details :

Hi,

Were Homewood Snowboards. Were a tight knit, family/friend operated snowboard manufacturer based out of Bradford, PA. We like road trips, tacos, chopoing wood, long converesations we aren’t going to remember, oh yeah.. and snowboarding.

Snowboarding has been a part of our lives since we were old enough to think for ourselves, and we love it with all our hearts. There aren’t many things that make us feel as good as we do when we tear up a mountain together. That’s why we started Homewood. We don’t think of this as just making snowboards, we thnk of it as giving everybody a way to feel as good as we do when we ride.

Like we said, our boards are made in PA, not by robot hands, but by our own high-five givin’, make a livin’ hands. Day in and day out we work the skin off our digits. Why you ask? Because this is our dream! Because we want to support the people and things we love. This is how we share our favorite things with the world.

Love,

Homewood.
